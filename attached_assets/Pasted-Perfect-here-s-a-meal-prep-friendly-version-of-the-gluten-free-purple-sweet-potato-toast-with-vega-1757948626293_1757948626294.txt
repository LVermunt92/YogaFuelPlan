Perfect — here’s a meal-prep friendly version of the gluten-free purple sweet potato toast with vegan feta. This way you can bake once, prep toppings in bulk, and assemble quickly during the week.

⸻

1. Gluten-Free Purple Sweet Potato Bread (batch)

Makes 1 loaf, ~8–10 slices

Ingredients
	•	400 g steamed purple sweet potato, mashed
	•	200 g rice flour
	•	120 g tapioca flour (or potato starch)
	•	50 g almond flour
	•	2 tbsp ground flaxseed + 6 tbsp water (2 flax “eggs”)
	•	10 g instant yeast
	•	1.5 tbsp sugar
	•	1 tsp salt
	•	3 tbsp olive or coconut oil
	•	220 ml warm plant milk (adjust for batter-like consistency)

Method
	1.	Prepare flax eggs (flaxseed + water, rest 5 min).
	2.	Mix dry ingredients: rice flour, tapioca flour, almond flour, yeast, sugar, salt.
	3.	Add sweet potato, flax eggs, oil, and warm plant milk. Mix to sticky dough/batter.
	4.	Transfer to greased loaf tin, smooth top.
	5.	Rise 45–60 min until puffed.
	6.	Bake 180 °C for 40–45 min. Cool fully.
	7.	Meal-prep tip: Slice once cooled, freeze in zip bags (2 slices per portion). Toast straight from freezer.

⸻

2. Vegan Feta (keeps 5–7 days in fridge)

Ingredients
	•	400 g firm tofu (2 blocks), pressed
	•	5 tbsp lemon juice
	•	3 tbsp apple cider vinegar
	•	3 tbsp olive oil
	•	2 tbsp nutritional yeast
	•	2 tsp salt
	•	1 tsp oregano

Method
	1.	Crumble tofu.
	2.	Mix marinade and coat tofu.
	3.	Store in airtight container in fridge. Drain excess liquid before using.

⸻

3. Greens & Protein Layers

Bulk sautéed greens
	•	3–4 cups spinach (or other greens)
	•	2 cups green peas
	•	2 tbsp olive oil
	•	Salt & pepper

Sauté quickly, cool, store in container (3–4 days in fridge).

Protein
	•	Vegetarian option: cook scrambled eggs fresh each day.
	•	Vegan option (make ahead):
	•	1 cup chickpea flour
	•	1 cup water
	•	½ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp salt, pinch black pepper
Mix and cook like scrambled eggs. Store 2–3 days in fridge, reheat gently.

⸻

4. Assembly (per portion)
	1.	Toast 2 slices purple sweet potato bread.
	2.	Spread ½ avocado, mashed with lemon + salt.
	3.	Add a scoop of greens & peas.
	4.	Layer scrambled eggs or chickpea scramble.
	5.	Sprinkle with vegan feta, roasted pumpkin seeds, black sesame.

⸻

✅ Prep once, enjoy all week:
	•	Bread (freeze slices).
	•	Vegan feta (fridge, 1 week).
	•	Greens (3–4 days).
	•	Chickpea scramble (2–3 days).
