Here is a simple, fully plant-based quiche with spinach and cherry tomatoes.

Ingredients (24 cm / 9.5 inch tart tin)

Crust
	•	200 g flour (wheat or spelt)
	•	80 g plant-based margarine (cold, in cubes)
	•	4–5 tbsp cold water
	•	½ tsp salt

(Quicker alternative: use 1 sheet of vegan puff pastry.)

Filling
	•	200 g firm tofu (pressed)
	•	250–300 g fresh spinach
	•	200 g cherry tomatoes, halved
	•	1 small onion, finely chopped
	•	2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
	•	3 tbsp nutritional yeast (optional, for a “cheesy” flavour)
	•	100 ml unsweetened plant-based milk
	•	1 tbsp cornstarch or chickpea flour
	•	1 tsp mustard
	•	½ tsp turmeric (for colour, optional)
	•	1 tsp dried thyme or Italian herbs
	•	Salt and pepper to taste
	•	Oil for frying

⸻

Method
	1.	Make the crust
	•	Preheat the oven to 180 °C / 355 °F (top and bottom heat).
	•	Mix the flour and salt, then rub in the margarine until crumbs form.
	•	Add 4–5 tbsp cold water until a firm dough comes together.
	•	Roll out and line a greased quiche/tart tin. Prick the base with a fork.
	•	Blind bake: pre-bake for 10 minutes (without filling).
	2.	Prepare the spinach
	•	Sauté the onion in a little oil until translucent. Add the garlic briefly.
	•	Add the spinach in batches and let it wilt.
	•	Drain in a colander and press out as much liquid as possible.
	3.	Tofu “egg” mixture
	•	Add the tofu, plant-based milk, cornstarch/chickpea flour, mustard, nutritional yeast, turmeric, herbs, salt and pepper to a blender or bowl.
	•	Blend with a blender or immersion blender until smooth.
	4.	Fill the quiche
	•	Stir the drained spinach into the tofu mixture.
	•	Spread this mixture over the pre-baked crust.
	•	Arrange the halved cherry tomatoes on top, cut side up, and press them in lightly.
	5.	Bake
	•	Bake the quiche for 30–40 minutes at 180 °C / 355 °F, until the filling is set and the top is lightly golden.
	•	Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.

⸻

Variations (optional)
	•	Extra protein: add 50–70 g smoked tofu in cubes or 3 tbsp cooked chickpeas.
	•	Herbs: replace thyme with oregano or herbes de Provence.
