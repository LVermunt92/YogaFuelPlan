Breakfast options (150–300 mg flavanols)

1. Cacao–Blueberry Power Oats
	•	Rolled oats or buckwheat flakes
	•	1 tbsp unsweetened cacao powder (+13 mg)
	•	½ cup blueberries (+5–10 mg)
	•	1 scoop protein powder
	•	1 tbsp almond or pecan butter
	•	Cinnamon + pinch of salt
	•	Drink: 1 cup green tea (+275 mg)

→ ~300 mg flavanols total, high in fiber, protein, and magnesium.

⸻

2. Cashew Yogurt Bowl with Berries & Cacao Nibs
	•	Unsweetened soy or cashew yogurt
	•	1 tbsp raw cacao nibs (+25 mg)
	•	½ cup blackberries (+30 mg)
	•	1 tbsp chia seeds
	•	Optional: drizzle of honey
	•	Drink: black tea (+250 mg)

→ ~300 mg flavanols, gut-friendly and anti-inflammatory.

⸻

3. Protein Smoothie
	•	1 tbsp cocoa powder (+13 mg)
	•	1 cup soy milk
	•	½ banana + handful frozen blueberries (+10 mg)
	•	1 scoop protein
	•	1 tsp flax or hempseed
	•	Drink: green tea (+275 mg)

→ ~250–300 mg flavanols.
