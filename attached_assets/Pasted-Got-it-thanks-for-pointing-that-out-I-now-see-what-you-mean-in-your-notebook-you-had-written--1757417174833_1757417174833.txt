6a. Burgers with Sweet Potato Fries and Salad

Servings: 2
Time: 30–35 minutes

Ingredients
	•	2–3 sweet potatoes (≈400 g)
	•	2 burger buns
	•	2 beef burgers (≈150 g each)
	•	1 egg
	•	2 tbsp breadcrumbs (≈20 g)
	•	1 clove garlic, finely chopped
	•	1 small eggplant (≈200 g), sliced or grilled
	•	1 zucchini (≈200 g), sliced or grilled
	•	50 g bean sprouts (taugé)
	•	1 tbsp olive oil (15 ml)
	•	2 handfuls of mixed salad or lettuce

Preparation
	1.	Cut sweet potatoes into wedges, toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 200°C for about 25 minutes until golden and tender.
	2.	Season burgers lightly with garlic, egg, and breadcrumbs (if using fresh mince, mix and shape into patties; if using pre-made burgers, just cook directly).
	3.	Grill or pan-fry the burgers until cooked through.
	4.	Grill eggplant and zucchini until tender.
	5.	Toast or warm the burger buns.
	6.	Assemble burgers with the patties, grilled vegetables, bean sprouts, and salad.
	7.	Serve with roasted sweet potato fries.

6b. Beyond Meat Burgers with Sweet Potato Fries and Salad (Gluten- & Lactose-Free)

Servings: 2
Time: 30–35 minutes

Ingredients
	•	2–3 sweet potatoes (≈400 g)
	•	2 gluten-free burger buns
	•	2 Beyond Meat burgers (≈113 g each)
	•	2 tbsp gluten-free breadcrumbs (≈20 g) (optional, if using Beyond Meat mince instead of ready patties)
	•	1 clove garlic, finely chopped
	•	1 small eggplant (≈200 g), sliced or grilled
	•	1 zucchini (≈200 g), sliced or grilled
	•	50 g bean sprouts (taugé)
	•	1 tbsp olive oil (15 ml)
	•	2 handfuls of mixed salad or lettuce

Preparation
	1.	Cut sweet potatoes into wedges, toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 200°C for about 25 minutes.
	2.	Cook Beyond Meat burgers in a pan with a little oil until golden and cooked through. (If using mince: mix with garlic and gluten-free breadcrumbs, then shape into patties first.)
	3.	Grill eggplant and zucchini until tender.
	4.	Toast the gluten-free buns.
	5.	Assemble burgers with patties, grilled vegetables, bean sprouts, and salad.
	6.	Serve with roasted sweet potato fries.
